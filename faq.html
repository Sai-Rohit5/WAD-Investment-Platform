Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions related to investments

1. What is a Mutual Fund?

A mutual fund collects money from many investors and invests it in stocks, bonds or securities.

2. What is the difference between low, medium and high risk?

Low risk gives stable returns, medium risk gives moderate returns, and high risk offers higher returns with more fluctuation.

3. How often should I invest?

Most advisors recommend investing monthly (SIP) for long-term growth.

4. What is a diversified portfolio?

A portfolio that includes different asset types, reducing overall risk.

5. Can I withdraw my funds anytime?

Yes, except for certain lock-in period investments like ELSS.
